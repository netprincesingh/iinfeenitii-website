About

Dorai Raj, always dropped Mithali at the ground for training. Mithali’s interest was dance and so she used to take Bharatnatyam classes. She was also very fond of sleeping. She used to sleep till late at home. Her father was in defence, so to teach mithali some discipline, he used to take mithali with her to the academy. She sat near the boundary of training ground and did her homework there, and after that, she used to bat there casually. When coach saw her footworks, strokeplay, he went to her father and told him if mithali would continue play cricket, then she would surely make him proud. Mithali had got the potential in her. In 1995, by the recommendation of Jyothi Prasad, she got admission to coach Sampath Kumar ‘s academy. When Sampat Kumar saw Mithali Batting, she told her father that if he would give his full support to her, then she would definitely play for India at the age of just 14. Her parents didn’t believed that. Mithali practiced cricket in week days and practiced dance in the weekends. For local mathes and tournaments, she had to leave dance classes for ten to fifteen days. As Mithali took some big leave in the dance classes, so her dance teacher told her parents that Mithali should choose one between dance and cricket. Mithali choose cricket insted of her passion, dance.
